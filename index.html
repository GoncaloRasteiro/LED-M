What is LED - M

An inventory system that will improve the organization of technological resources.

Inventory

A centralized system to catalog and organize all school equipment, ensuring better control and visibility over resources such as computers, projectors, and laboratory materials.

Acess to Schools

Schools will have a streamlined way to check the availability of equipment, reducing confusion and ensuring that all resources are utilized efficiently across different departments and classrooms.

Requests

The system will include a feature for teachers and staff to submit requests for equipment usage, enabling easy reservations and tracking of borrowed items, while also avoiding conflicts or overbooking of resources.
